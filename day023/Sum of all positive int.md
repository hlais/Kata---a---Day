Day #23: 

Difficulty: 8 kyu

Solved: Thursday , February 1st, 2018

Description:

You get an array of numbers, return the sum of all of the positives ones.

Example [1,-4,7,12] => 1 + 7 + 12 = 20

Note: array may be empty, in this case return 0.
